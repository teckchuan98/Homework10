Number of Holidays in Asian Countries

This visualisation demonstrates the number of holidays in each Asian Countries.

The Bar

The bar chart categorises the Asian countries into categories based on the number of holidays of each country. Low category ranging from 0 to 12, Medium category ranging from 13 to 17 whereas High category ranging from 18 to infinity.

Holidays in 50 States of USA

From the plot to the right, most of the states in USA fall under the medium category which range from 12 to 17. Meanwhile from the high category, it is shown that Texas has the highest number of holidays whereas, a few other states such as Minnesota and South Dakota share the low number of holidays
